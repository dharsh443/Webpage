About Us

The Glam House Beauty Salon

Beauty Anywhere Anytime

The Glam House Beauty Salon is your one-stop beauty destination curated by experts to offer the best in hair, skin, and beauty services. Our Galm House mission is to deliver premium and luxury quality service with our professionally & technically qualified team.

The world-class equipment products that we use are of premium quality and we maintain the highest level of cleanliness and hygiene. Our therapists and consultants always walk that extra mile to ensure that you are comfortable and leave you with a sense of satisfaction and a relaxing experience after every service and we keep you make the mark to be the destination for top-to-toe beauty service always.

Our alluring bridal studio has an exclusive space for bridal makeup with necessary makeup kits, hairstyling products and very good lighting facility, which is suitable for the bride to get the required privacy and personalized service.
